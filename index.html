Hi there, my name is Asia Bragg

I'm a programmer, data analyst and digital artist operating in Southern California.

I absolutely love to program and I absolutely love to make art.


I've been programming since my dad introduced me to the concept at six years old. Since that young age I've battled with the idea that I'd have to pick just one career path and abandon the other.
However, one day it came to me like all epiphanies do... Why not both? (Shoutout to all the people who get the commercial reference!) Long story short I'm a web/software developer as well as a digital artist/animator. Lets collab :)
